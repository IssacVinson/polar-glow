1. Introduction

Polar Glow Detailing (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We use your information to create/manage your account, process bookings and payments, communicate about services, provide customer support, and comply with legal obligations.

We use industry-standard security (Firebase security rules, encrypted connections). No system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data from the app settings at any time. You may also request data deletion (subject to legal requirements).

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
